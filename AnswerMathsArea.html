First , let us find the area of both the circles
∵ Area of 1 circle = (22/7) * 14 * 14 which is equal to 616 cm 2
∴ Area of 2 circles = 2 * 616 cm 2 = 1232 cm 2.

Now , let us find the area of the rectangle .
If we observe closely , we find that :-
  1. The diameter of 1 circle is equal to the breadth of the rectangle.
  2. The diameters of both the circles are equal to the length of the rectangle.
∴ We can say that breadth = 28 cm and length = 56 cm.
Now , since we know the length and breadth of the rectangle , we can calculate its area.
Area = l * b = 56 * 28 = 1568 cm 2.

Now , since we know the areas of both the 2D shapes , we can subtract their areas to obtain the area of the shaded portion.
The area of the shaded portion = 1568 cm 2 - 1232 cm 2


∴ The area of the shaded portion = 336 cm 2
